## 🛠️ The Analytical Workflow

### 🔧 1. Feature Engineering & Tuning
* **Noise Extraction:** Isolated and treated missing data fields, normalizing currency metrics and high-variance mileage values.
* **Depreciation Triggers:** Evaluated correlations between vehicle age, brand equity, structural damages, and overall market lifespan.

### 📉 2. Exploratory Data Discovery (EDA)
* Identified specific vehicle categories that retain maximum equity over standard $3\text{-year}$ ownership matrices.
* Charted linear and non-linear patterns mapping how extreme mileage spikes affect individual brand segments.
